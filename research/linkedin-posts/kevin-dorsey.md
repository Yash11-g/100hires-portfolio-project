Kevin Dorsey - LinkedIn Research

Expert Overview

Kevin Dorsey is a B2B SaaS sales leader, revenue strategist, and founder of KD Advisory. He has held executive leadership roles at companies including PatientPop and ServiceTitan, where he focused on building scalable sales organizations and predictable revenue systems. Kevin regularly shares insights on sales leadership, outbound strategy, coaching, pipeline generation, and revenue operations.

⸻

Primary Content Themes

1. Building Repeatable Sales Systems

Kevin emphasizes that consistent sales success comes from repeatable processes rather than relying on individual talent. He encourages organizations to document best practices, measure performance, and continuously optimize their outbound strategy.

For B2B SaaS companies, repeatable systems improve forecasting, onboarding, and long-term revenue growth.

⸻

2. Data-Driven Sales Decisions

A recurring theme throughout Kevin’s content is the importance of measuring sales activities and outcomes. Rather than relying on assumptions, he advocates using metrics such as conversion rates, reply rates, meeting quality, and pipeline health to improve performance.

This approach enables sales teams to identify bottlenecks and make informed adjustments.

⸻

3. Coaching Over Managing

Kevin believes that successful sales leaders spend more time coaching than supervising. Regular feedback, call reviews, and skill development create stronger teams than simply tracking activity metrics.

This philosophy helps organizations build long-term sales capability rather than short-term performance.

⸻

Key Takeaways

* Build repeatable outbound systems instead of relying on individual performers.
* Measure activities and outcomes to improve decision-making.
* Coaching is one of the highest-leverage responsibilities of sales leadership.
* Continuous experimentation leads to sustainable pipeline growth.
* Strong sales cultures prioritize learning, accountability, and consistency.

⸻

Practical Applications

B2B SaaS organizations can apply Kevin Dorsey’s principles by documenting outbound workflows, reviewing sales conversations regularly, tracking key pipeline metrics, and creating structured coaching programs for SDRs and Account Executives.

These practices improve onboarding, increase forecasting accuracy, and create a more scalable revenue engine.
