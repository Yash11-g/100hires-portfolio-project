Morgan Ingram - LinkedIn Research

Expert Overview

Morgan Ingram is the founder and CEO of AMP Creative, a B2B growth consultancy specializing in modern sales development, social selling, and personal branding. He previously served in leadership roles at companies such as JB Sales and has become one of the most recognized voices in Sales Development Representative (SDR) enablement. His content focuses on prospecting, video outreach, LinkedIn selling, pipeline generation, and helping sales professionals build authentic relationships with buyers.

⸻

Primary Content Themes

1. Social Selling Creates Better Conversations

Morgan consistently teaches that buyers engage more with sales professionals who establish credibility before reaching out. Rather than relying solely on cold emails or calls, he encourages SDRs to build a visible presence on LinkedIn by sharing insights, commenting thoughtfully, and engaging with prospects before initiating outreach.

This approach increases familiarity and improves response rates.

⸻

2. Video Prospecting Improves Engagement

One of Morgan’s most recognizable themes is the use of personalized video messages. He demonstrates how short, relevant videos can capture attention more effectively than traditional text-based outreach while helping sales representatives build trust earlier in the buying process.

For B2B SaaS companies, video prospecting differentiates outreach in competitive markets.

⸻

3. Consistency Beats Perfection

Morgan frequently emphasizes that successful prospecting comes from consistent daily execution rather than searching for the perfect script or sequence. Continuous learning, experimentation, and repetition help SDRs improve performance over time.

He encourages sales professionals to review their outreach regularly and refine messaging based on real-world feedback.

⸻

Key Takeaways

* Build credibility before asking for meetings.
* Social selling complements traditional outbound strategies.
* Personalized video messages increase engagement and response rates.
* Consistency and daily practice outperform occasional bursts of activity.
* Authentic communication builds stronger long-term customer relationships.

⸻

Practical Applications

B2B SaaS sales teams can apply Morgan Ingram’s principles by integrating LinkedIn engagement into prospecting workflows, using personalized videos for key accounts, and encouraging SDRs to develop personal brands that reinforce credibility with potential buyers.

Sales leaders can also create coaching programs focused on communication quality rather than simply measuring activity volume.
