Cold Outreach Pipeline for B2B SaaS

Research Objective

The objective of this project is to identify and analyze leading practitioners in B2B SaaS cold outreach and outbound sales.

This research focuses on understanding modern prospecting frameworks, cold email strategies, LinkedIn outreach, sales psychology, multi-channel sequencing, and SDR best practices.

The final goal is to extract recurring patterns and actionable frameworks that can be used to build effective outbound systems.

⸻

Selection Criteria

Experts were selected based on the following criteria:

* Proven experience in B2B SaaS sales or revenue leadership
* Active content creation and thought leadership
* Practical and actionable advice
* Strong reputation among sales practitioners
* Demonstrated success in outbound sales and prospecting
